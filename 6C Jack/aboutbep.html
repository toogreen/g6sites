The Black Eyed Peas are a American hip hop group and their music is influenced by pop, dance, electro and techno.The Black Eyed Peas started from LA and California in 1995. However, they were not very popular after debut.
It was the third album "Elephunk", which is released in 2003, brought the worldwide sucess. From that time, the Black Eyed Peas' music including "Boom Boom Pow", "Don't Phunk With My Heart", "Where is the love" and "Shut up" achieved a worldwide sucess.
Also, the Black Eyed Peas has sold 56 million (56 000 000) records all around the world. They also became the second best selling group for the download tracks, with over 42 million sales in 2011.
