width="132" height="23" alt""/> Jinchuuriki's are the ninjas that have tailed beasts sealed inside there body. There goal is to master the skills of tailed beasts and communicate with them. This generation of jinchurikis(picture at the left)have all completed taking contact and earning their power. They have been ignored by the villagers beacuse of their beasts inside. Every Jinchurikis have a sad early life. Tailed beasts are monsters that have numerous tails. Starting from One-tails, they have different powers. Nine-tails is the strongest of all. Akatsuki tries to capture them and make them into a beast that will be able to destroy the whole world, which is called Ten-tails. Ten-tails was turned into 9 beasts in order to make the world more safe by the sixpath sage. They all can use a technique called tailed-beast bomb, which can destroy mountains.
